Personal Training

A personal approach to reaching your fitness goal

I've been a Personal Trainer now for over 10 years. I love a challenge to see just how far I can push myself physically and mentally! I also enjoy helping my clients push past their limit and do things that they thought were impossible before training with me.

I now make the most of every day because I feel that every day is a gift for me. So I try to have as many adventures as I can

In January 2021 I was diagnosed with cancer. The news shocked me and my family but I wasn't about to quit. I used my mindset to stay strong and pull me though the operation (to remove a 4.5 inch tumour) and chemo. In August 2021 after finishing chemo, I was told that my markers were back down to normal. My family have always been strong and helped me through. This is were i get the strength to support others and help them achieve their goals. No matter how big or scary the goal, Keep Moving Forward!
